Safety at Workplace Declaration

I understand that all the workplace accidents, hazards and illness can be prevented by following the safety guidelines properly. I hereby declare that I shall be abiding the following safety procedure at my workplace.

  • I will be working with proper safety gears – Helmets, Aprons, Uniforms, Safety Shoes, Gloves, Eye Gears, Safety Masks- Gas mask, welding masks as required for my job and at my workplace.
  • I will be working sincerely and cautiously at my workplace to maintain complete safety.
  • I will ensure complete workplace safety under all circumstances
  • I will not operate any machine or any device without authorization and permission by my Supervisor and Factory Admin.
  • I will not conduct any action which is unsafe for myself, my co-worker or impedes normal work environment.
  • I will take full responsibility of my safety at workplace and will use all necessary protection.
  • I will not access any article / chemicals/ sharp or pointed objects / Wires etc. which can be potentially unsafe without proper permission and guidance from my Factory Admin and Floor Supervisor
  • As a work- Floor manager/ Floor Supervisor I will take care of entire safety of all my workmen.
  • I shall participate actively in the safety drills and safety training sessions conducted by my Organisation.

I hereby declare that I have received the safety guidance and have understood the same. I shall abide the safety guidelines and will maintain zero compromise with workplace safety. I also understand that any deviation from these rules shall be considered as a breach of safety discipline and will be subjected actions as deemed fit by Management.
